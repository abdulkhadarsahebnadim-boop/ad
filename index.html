Andar Bahar Game Rules

Learn how to play the most popular Indian card game

1

Choose Your Side

Select between Andar (left side) or Bahar (right side) before the game starts

2

Dealer Draws Cards

The dealer draws cards one by one, alternating between Andar and Bahar sides

3

Match the Suit

When a card with the same suit as the first card appears on your chosen side, you win!

4

Win 1:1 Odds

If your chosen side matches first, you win with 1:1 odds - double your bet amount!
